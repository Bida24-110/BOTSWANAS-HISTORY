Hi! I'm Tiko the Meerkat 🐾. Let's explore Botswana's amazing past!

📜 Pre-Colonial Times

Before 1800s, the Tswana people lived in chiefdoms with rich cultures and cattle-based economies.

🇬🇧 British Protectorate (1885)

Botswana (then Bechuanaland) became a British protectorate to avoid colonization by neighbors.

🎉 Independence (1966)

On Sept 30, 1966, Botswana became an independent nation with Seretse Khama as its first president!
